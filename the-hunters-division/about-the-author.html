About the Author

Julie L. Schorr is the author of The Hunters Division series and upcoming related works set in the same universe. She was born 1990 in Louisville, KY, where she still lives. She prefers writing low fantasy or sci-fantasy with semi-dark undertones. She believes writing what you know is best, and that by writing from the heart, you can be your own therapist. If no one else is around, pick up a notebook or find a computer, because you are always there for yourself.

Book One: The Demonics

Major Langdon Hughes, leader of the Hunters Division, attempts to find a way to kill demonics, but no weapon, no technique, has proven successful.

Time is ticking down, and the Chapters in Chicago and New York are ready to make an offensive attack on the Fulsera, witches and wizards that caused the destruction of the world. They call out for a Merge of cities under one banner, one goal.

Hughes isn't ready to abandon his job yet and makes one last effort, putting everything on the line. All for one last chance...

Book Two: The Fulsera

Captain Milton Aleksandrs of the Hunters Division deals with the confusing aftermath of the lockdown at the Jefferson Chapter, and the Merge is finally accepted, prompting several Hunters to journey to Chicago.

Unexpected alliances are formed, prophecies are revealed, and connections are discovered when it comes to demonics.

Book Three: The Immortals

Demonics have returned and plague the world once again with their presence, now fully imitating the human appearance. Fear grips not only Nulsera, but Fulsera and Immortals as well.

No one is free, no one can run, and no one can hide.

It's a waiting game for all, and not everyone will survive, for the Judge has seen all, and she has awakened.

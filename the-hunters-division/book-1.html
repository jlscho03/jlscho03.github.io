Book One: The Demonics

Major Langdon Hughes, leader of the Hunters Division, attempts to find a way to kill demonics, but no weapon, no technique, has proven successful.

Time is ticking down, and the Chapters in Chicago and New York are ready to make an offensive attack on the Fulsera, witches and wizards that caused the destruction of the world. They call out for a Merge of cities under one banner, one goal.

Hughes isn't ready to abandon his job yet and makes one last effort, putting everything on the line. All for one last chance...

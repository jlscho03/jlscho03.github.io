What's Next after the Hunters Division?

While this story has been written, the author still has several story ideas set in th same universe. There are roughly 35 story ideas, of which 30 take place in the same universe. These include stories ranging from prequels, the Fulseran perspective, and expanded stories for side characters or side events mentioned in the first series.

However, due to the nature of Book Three's extensive editing, a break is going to be taken from The Hunter's Division upon it's completion to work on a shorter work set in a new universe.

Idagio is the first story in The Crystal Song series following 11-year-old Noa Hempworth in a classic fantasy world aimed for a potential middle-grade audience. Work on the novelette's first draft will begin early January of 2016 with a goal completion date of late April of 2016.

Idagio

Disclaimer: Plot and tone subject to change during the course of actually writing the novellette.

11-year-old Noa Hempworth lives on the outskirts of the Empire's lands with her adoptive parents. When a wolf emerges from the woods nearby, she quickly befriends it, but in doing so, she finds herself being isolated at school, her friends abandoning her. The wolf, Idagio, proves to be a good friend for Noa, always being there during the arguments her parents have. But when the Empire starts tracking students for potential military potential, Idagio also proves to be a loyal guardian. But can the wolf-dog Idagio match an Empire official's wolf-dog, Thatch?

Introduction

The Hunters Division is but one story in a series of many revolving around the rise and downfall of the Alseran, corresponding with the rise and fall of the Fulsera. Here, you may find more information relating to the world and its historical and future events, characters, organizations, and regions, many of which expand upon the world introduced in The Hunters Division.

Spoilers are present, so explore at your own risk.

The author retains all right to the world and its characters and events. The purpose of this Wikia is to allow an easy point of reference for the author and for any that may be interested in the world.

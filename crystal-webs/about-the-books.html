An Introduction

Eight billion human casualties. The Dex should have won the war, but somehow, humanity prevailed. Only a million people survived, but through the ashes of a broken Earth, the peaceful nation of Apharis arose. A new age for humanity had begun.

But that was two centuries ago, so unless itâ€™s history class, Vicki, Amanda, and Val have more important things to deal with. The only thing they have in common is their house at the University of Cadfield; Vicki is a quiet loner who cares more about making art than friends, and Amanda is a stuck-up ice queen. But the only thing that matters more to Val than her history degree is making new friends. Sure, her roommates came from different backgrounds and would have never hung out in high school, but college is a new era for them. How hard could it be to put the past to bed?

In their struggle to get along, they stumble onto something greater than their own problems. These college girls hold the key to Apharis' secrets. They hold the truth behind the Dex and their war on humanity. Everything could change.

About the Author

Matthew L. Saunders is the author of Crystal Webs and upcoming related works set in the same universe. He was born 1994 in Reno, NV, where he was raised. He prefers writing character-based science fiction stories with a focus on slice-of-life stories. He is a reknowned vegan and coffee adict, cat lover and crazed fan of jumping spiders. In the future, he hopes to be a sponsor for Google and has quite the affinity for their products and services. So, now that the facts have been given, one may find Matthew in the library on a gloomy, cloudy day, snuggling with a cat he smuggled into the library, his hand wrapped around a steaming mug of coffee, the other on his Chromebook typing away or producing artwork.
